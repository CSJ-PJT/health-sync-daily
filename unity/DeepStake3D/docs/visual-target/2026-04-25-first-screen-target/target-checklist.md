# Target Checklist

Use this checklist before calling a first-screen pass successful.

## Composition

- player is visible within 3 seconds
- route from player to notice board is immediately readable
- notice board is the first clear objective landmark
- buildings frame the objective instead of burying it

## Landmark

- notice board silhouette is strong
- notice board has dark frame/backing
- notice board has a clear grounded base
- local contrast around the notice board reads at screenshot scale

## Buildings

- roof mass reads separately from wall mass
- wall mass reads separately from base/foundation
- entrance areas are visible
- windows are visible as larger accents
- buildings do not collapse into one dark brown mass

## Ground

- ground is continuous
- ground is calm and secondary
- path is readable without becoming a giant carpet
- no strong woodgrain/ring/grid dominance
- no broad color-carpet look

## Background

- top/right background hides the test-map grid quietly
- no giant rounded blob hills
- background blockers stay low and non-dominant

## Avoid

- visible grid/tile sheet ground
- broad terrain carpets
- dark brown building mass
- tiny facade details invisible from camera
- random clutter
