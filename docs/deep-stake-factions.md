# Deep Stake Factions

## Purpose

This document defines the major faction baseline for the active `DeepStake3D` production path.

All factions are fictional science-fantasy entities or networks.
They should not be treated as literal real-world groups.

## Major antagonistic faction

### Continuum Directorate

Role:
- the main systemic antagonist during the grounded modern-era phase

Public face:
- infrastructure recovery funds
- debt restructuring boards
- logistics stabilization offices
- research and archive trusts
- emergency relief partnerships

Hidden function:
- captures land through dependency and debt
- redirects supplies to controlled corridors
- reshapes records and information flows
- benefits from weakened settlements and lowered resonance
- prolongs fear and instability because collapse improves leverage

Pressure domains:
- land
- debt
- logistics
- media fog
- information control
- resonance suppression

## Integration points

### World design

- recovery fields under lien pressure
- service roads with missing shipments
- archive files with altered ownership chains
- settlements pressured into dependency contracts

### Quest design

- debt and archive document recovery
- land claim disputes
- supply route restoration
- exposed relief fraud or manipulated records

### Settlement systems

- influence pressure against independent beacons
- slower recovery if routes stay compromised
- allegiance shifts among local NPCs depending on how the player responds

### Alignment and resonance

- resisting Directorate pressure improves compassion, clarity, and resonance stability
- exploitative choices may reduce immediate friction but increase corruption pressure and dependence

### Late-game cosmology

The Continuum Directorate eventually connects to larger anti-transition forces.
It is not the final metaphysical antagonist by itself, but it acts as a worldly instrument of delayed awakening and dimensional resistance.

## Supportive factions already in scope

- Luminous Companions
- Deep Archive
- Dawnkeepers
- Serpent Court
- Shadow Administration remnants

These should remain clearly fictional and should evolve through the same in-world logic.
