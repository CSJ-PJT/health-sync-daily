# Asset Placement Guide

Use this guide to place and apply environment assets without redesigning the game map.

## Placement principles

1. Preserve current layout.
2. Improve material identity first.
3. Add props only where they clarify function.
4. Keep player routes readable.
5. Do not fill empty space with clutter.
6. Keep interaction markers visible.
7. Maintain quarter/isometric camera readability.

## Zone identity

### Recovery Field
Purpose: main safe hub.

Recommended visuals:
- mixed grass/dirt ground
- compact dirt paths
- warm campfire center
- simple crates and benches
- clinic and archive readable from camera

Do not over-clutter the central path.

### Clinic Zone
Purpose: recovery, treatment, health sync conversion.

Recommended visuals:
- worn concrete entrance
- clean but aged wall material
- medical sign board
- water tank
- simple supply crates
- subtle warm windows

Keep it cleaner than other zones.

### Archive Zone
Purpose: records, missions, reports, lore.

Recommended visuals:
- wood + brick material mix
- notice board
- document crates
- dim warm window lighting
- less metal, more storage identity

### Workshop Zone
Purpose: crafting, repair, upgrades.

Recommended visuals:
- gravel or compact dirt ground
- corrugated metal walls
- barrels, generator, tool racks
- darker roof
- visible industrial material separation

This zone can be slightly more cluttered, but paths must stay clear.

### Farm / Greenhouse
Purpose: food, water, recovery resources.

Recommended visuals:
- farm soil rows
- grass borders
- greenhouse frame
- water tank
- wooden fence
- small crops

Avoid making it look like a fantasy farm.

### City Checkpoint
Purpose: urban entry, danger, permits, supplies.

Recommended visuals:
- worn asphalt road
- cracked asphalt near damaged areas
- concrete sidewalk
- reinforced concrete walls
- metal fences and sandbags
- aged metal signs

This should be more severe than the village, but still readable.

## Prop density guide

- Recovery hub: low-medium
- Clinic: low
- Archive: medium-low
- Workshop: medium
- Farm: medium-low
- City checkpoint: medium

## Lighting guide

- Use soft daylight base.
- Add warm local lights only for windows, clinic, archive, campfire.
- Use contact shadows under buildings and props.
- Avoid heavy fog.
- Avoid strong bloom.

## Final rule
Every added asset should either:

1. clarify zone function,
2. improve material quality,
3. improve navigation/readability,
4. or support the survival recovery mood.

If it does none of these, do not add it.
